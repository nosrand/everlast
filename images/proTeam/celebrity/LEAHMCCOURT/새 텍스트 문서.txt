LEAH MCCOURT
PROFESSIONAL FEATHERWEIGHT MMA FIGHTER

ABOUT
“The Blessed Curse” is an Irish MMA prospect in the women’s featherweight division. Currently fighting for a top promoter in Bellator, Leah has already made history by headlining her own card in Dublin, Ireland. She has not lost a bout since dropping her debut fight, and continues to keep her win streak alive while also being one of the major pioneers of MMA in her native Ireland.
