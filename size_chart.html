  • ECOMMERCE MANAGER
    JOB DESCRIPTION:
    1. This role oversees the end-to-end e-commerce marketing and merchandising activities for the ecommerce business.
    2. The ideal candidate is highly organized with a proven ability to multi-task especially in an agile, fast-paced, deadline driven environment. He or she should have a strong attention to detail and the ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast paced environment.
    DESIRED SKILLS & EXPERIENCE:
    1. At least 5 years of professional experience with at least 3 years of experience ecommerce managing marketing and merchandising programs.
    2. Technical understanding of e-commerce web platforms and analytics. Magento experience a must.
    3. Competency with HTML and CSS relative to CMS preferably Magento.
    4. Understanding of front and back-end logistical online order placement and fulfillment cycles (payment process, back-end inventory fulfillment, order confirmation and shipping) to optimize online customer experience.
    5. Experience constructing online marketing/advertising programs and site content.
    6. Digital Marketing experience SEO, SEM, Affiliate, etc.with a heavy emphasis on email marketing.
    7. Email Marketing experience with HTML coding, list management and email analytics are a must with a preference using the Bronto and Klaviyo email service providers.
    DAY TO DAY:
    1. Manage site calendar regarding seasonal product launches, site enhancements, promotions, etc.
    2. Manage all site content including daily management of home and category pages ensuring key goals are achieved.
    3. Lead the delivery and trafficking of creative assets to/from site/graphic designers and developers.
    4. Partner with third party partners to optimize opportunities to grow marketing programs and customer audience.
    5. Monitor and report on key campaign metrics including open rates, conversions, CTR, and retention.
    6. Job Type: Full-time
    EXPERIENCE:
    1. Merchandising: 2 years (Preferred)
    2. Email Marketing: 3 years
    3. E-Commerce: 5 years (Preferred)
  • GLOBAL LICENSING COORDINATOR
    DESCRIPTION:

    The Global Licensing Coordinator is responsible for providing departmental support for all facets of the Everlast Global Licensing Program including day to day maintenance of all licensing department reports/processes/forms/contact sheets, contract compliance, trademark infringement/protection, forecasting, royalty reporting, payments,and collections, and will interact with the licensees for the Everlast Global brand portfolio daily. The Global Licensing Coordinator role will also include the implementation of processes and initiatives to ensure cohesive, consistent worldwide brand presence, requiring strict attention to and enforcement of trademark registration requirements to ensure protection of the IBML trademarks. This position is based in Miami, FL.

    RESPONSIBILITIES:
    1. Provide support in a broad range of compliance and operational issues/areas, assisting in the collection of licensees’ Annual Forecasts, Royalty Reports & Royalty Payments.
    2. Prepare Licensee Annual Business Review templates and assist with preparing Annual Forecasts.
    3. Maintain the Master Licensee List, Contract Summary Information Sheets, Contract Expiration Report, and Licensee Contact Lists
    4. Manage the website Domain Registration and/or Transfer processes.
    5. Coordinate Global Licensing Meetings together with Licensing Brand Managers &Marketing Team.
    6. Assist Brand Manager & Directors with creation of New Business Plan templates for all new licensee business.
    7. Create Welcome Kits for new licensees, providing Brand Guidelines & assistance in establishing access to Everlast’Brandfolder assets.
    8. Assist in the collection of Trademark Proof of Use && the Trademark registration processes.
    9. Coordinate and assist with Trademark infringement matters reported by Licensees.
    10. Draft all Letters of Authorization for Licensees&Manufacturers.
    11. Provide support to the licensing team.
    QUALIFICATIONS:
    1. Strong project management and execution skills.
    2. Highly detailed with strong organizational capabilities.
    3. Clear written and oral communication skills.
    4. Demonstrates a strong desire to exceed expectations inherent with the position.
    5. Excels in a team environment, demonstrates an upbeat attitude, and ability to work effectively and professionally.
    6. Bachelor’s degree or significant relevant experience preferred.
    7. Proficient Computer Knowledge and user of Microsoft Office, Work, Teams, Excel, PowerPoint, Excel, and Word.
    8. Ability to perform under deadlines and in a stressful environment.
    9. Collaboration-oriented, with ability to work independently.
    10. Proficiency in a foreign language a plus.
